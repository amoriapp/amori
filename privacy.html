AI Chatbot (Amori Bot) and Conversation-Based Learning

Amori utilizes an AI chatbot called "Amori Bot" within the App that learns and adapts based on user conversations. The Amori Bot analyzes user interactions, preferences, and behaviors to provide personalized experiences and dating advice, and to improve the quality of matches. Please note the following privacy implications related to the Amori Bot:

  • Data Collection: The Amori Bot may collect and analyze information shared during conversations with users, including personal preferences, interests, and other relevant data, to optimize the user experience and improve match suggestions. Furthermore, by pressing “I Allow” when Amori Bot asks if it can know about your conversations, the user also allows Amori Bot to collect information shared during conversations with other users.
  • Personalization: The Amori Bot uses the collected data to tailor recommendations and suggestions within the App. It may utilize machine learning techniques to understand user preferences better and provide more relevant content and matches.
  • Data Protection: We take the privacy and security of your data seriously. Any personal information shared during conversations will be handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We have implemented appropriate safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
  • Anonymized and Aggregated Data: To further improve our services, we may anonymize and aggregate the data collected through the Amori Bot for analytical and statistical purposes. This ensures that individual users cannot be personally identified from the processed data during our research and development.
  • User Control: You have control over the information you share with the Amori Bot and with other users. You can choose the extent to which you engage in conversations and provide personal details. You can also request the deletion of your data as outlined in the "Data Retention & Deletion" section of this Privacy Policy.

User Control: Opting Out of Amori Bot Data Collection

We understand that some users may prefer not to have their conversations contribute to the data collection process for the Amori Bot. When users first get on the app, they can say “Maybe later” to the Amori Bot, and it will not collect data from their conversations with other users. We also provide an option to opt out of data collection from conversations through the "Amori Bot Learning" toggle in the Account section of the Settings page on the App. By disabling this toggle, you can choose not to have your conversations used for further learning and improvement of the AI algorithm. Please note that opting out of data collection from conversations may impact the personalized experience and match suggestions provided by the Amori Bot. However, other functionalities of the App will remain unaffected by this choice.

Please be aware that while we strive to make the Amori Bot experience as accurate and helpful as possible, it may not always provide perfect matches or recommendations. The bot's suggestions are based on available data and algorithms, and its understanding may not fully capture the nuances of human communication.

Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your personal information only as long as we need it for legitimate business purposes and as permitted by applicable law. If you decide to stop using our services, you are able to close your account, and your profile will stop being visible to other users.

To protect the safety and security of our users, we implement a safety retention window of 3 months. It is possible to restore your account during this period in case it was accidentally deleted or deactivated. During this period, we keep your information in the event that it might be necessary to investigate unlawful or harmful conducts. The retention of information during this safety retention window is based on our legitimate interest as well as that of potential third-party victims. After the retainment period, we delete your data and only keep limited information for specified purposes, as laid out below:

  • We maintain limited data to comply with legal data retention obligations. In particular, we keep transaction data (if you make purchases within the app) for 10 years to comply with tax and accounting legal requirements. We may also need to keep “traffic data” / logs for one year to comply with legal data retention obligations. We also keep records of consents users give us for five years to evidence our compliance with applicable law.
  • We maintain limited information for legitimate business interests such as data necessary to prevent members who were banned from opening a new account, for as long as necessary to ensure the safety and vital interests of our members.
  • We maintain information in cases where there is an outstanding or potential issue, claim or dispute requiring us to keep information (in particular if we receive a valid legal subpoena or request asking us to preserve data (in which case we would need to keep the data to comply with our legal obligations) or if data would otherwise be necessary as part of legal proceedings).

If you believe that the information we hold about you is inaccurate or that we are no longer entitled to use it and want to request its rectification, deletion, object to or restrict its processing, you can submit a request for the deletion of personal information by contacting us at info@amori.app. For your protection and the protection of all of our users, we may need to request specific information from you to help us confirm your identity before we can answer the above requests.
